THE PROCESS WITH BRETTE:

THOUGHTFUL CONNECTION

We begin with a conversation: not just about your wedding day, but about your family, your priorities, and how you want this weekend to be remembered.

CONSCIOUS PLANNING

Your film coverage is thoughtfully planned alongside your planner and creative team to ensure a seamless experience. Every decision is made with guest experience and flow in mind.

THE WEDDING DAY

On the wedding weekend, we move like guests and making sure to never be in the way. Our approach is unobtrusive, allowing moments to happen naturally to be captured.
